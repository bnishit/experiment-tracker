For Supabase, you need to get the complete connection string:

1. Go to your Supabase project dashboard
2. Click on "Project Settings" (gear icon)
3. Go to "Database" section
4. Scroll to "Connection string" 
5. Select "URI" mode (not "Session" or "Transaction")
6. Copy the full string - it should look like:
   postgresql://postgres.[PROJECT-REF]:[YOUR-PASSWORD]@aws-0-[REGION].pooler.supabase.com:6543/postgres?sslmode=require

OR for direct connection:
   postgresql://postgres:[YOUR-PASSWORD]@db.peyovrwqjjhgjivnbjlm.supabase.co:5432/postgres?sslmode=require

Replace [YOUR-PASSWORD] with your actual database password.

The format must be:
postgresql://[user]:[password]@[host]:[port]/[database]?sslmode=require

Update your .env file with the complete connection string.
